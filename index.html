About

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Informatics at Penn State University's College of IST, working under Dr. Syed Billah at the a11y lab. I bring 8+ years of combined experience — 3+ in industry and 5+ in Ph.D. research — across distributed systems, applied ML, and full-stack development.

My current research focuses on leveraging generative AI for human-computer interaction — using RAG and parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) to adapt LLMs to user needs. This builds on earlier work in accessibility and interaction design: custom input devices, interface adaptations, and probabilistic models of non-visual usability spanning desktop, mobile, and VR.

My first-author publications have appeared in CHI, UIST, ASSETS, IMWUT, and DIS — with two Best Paper Honorable Mentions (UIST'24, DIS'25).
